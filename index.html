Illja Rusakov

I am currently a first-year PhD student at Leipzig University under the supervision of Prof. Łukasz Grabowski. I am a member of the International Max Planck Research School within the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences.

My main mathematical passion lies in geometric group theory, in particular

In 2025, I completed my Master's degree in Mathematics at the University of Regensburg. My thesis, titled Logarithmic Torsion Homology Growth and Computability, was supervised by Prof. Clara Löh. It explored group-homological invariant torsion growth and analyzed its behavior under free and direct products.
